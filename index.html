Overview

RealScan is a Chrome browser extension that detects whether content is AI-generated. This privacy policy explains what data the Extension accesses, how it is used, and what is stored.

RealScan does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal information. We do not operate any servers or databases and have no ability to identify who you are or track your browsing activity.

Data Stored Locally

The following data is stored exclusively on your device using Chrome's local storage (chrome.storage.local) and never leaves your device except as described below:

API Key Your Google Gemini API key, saved only so you don't have to re-enter it on each use.
Scan Mode Your preferred scan mode (Text, Image, or Auto), saved as a local preference.

Data Sent to Third Parties

When you initiate a scan, the content you select (text or image) is sent directly from your browser to the Google Gemini API for AI detection analysis. This transmission is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

RealScan does not intercept, log, or store this data at any point. No other third parties receive any data.

Children's Privacy

RealScan is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from children.
